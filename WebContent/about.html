Jack My Tour

Jack My Tour is a web application that allows one to plan their next trip.

This web app helps the user create an agenda based on their needs. Whether it be restaurants, music, sports, outdoor events, or even historical places – Jack My Tour provides its users with the most potent information to make their trip easy, organized, and packed with fun.

Jack My Tour provides flexibility to its users. One can spontaneously decide their activities for the day – or plan well in advance where they want to go. An agenda is formulated out of the activities chosen and Jack my Tour will best decide how to fit in everything into the trip. The user can save their newly created agenda for the for later viewing. It is possible to edit and change the agenda to best fit the users trip. Jack my tour is an all around travel app that can help make travel life easier.
